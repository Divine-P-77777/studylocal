# StudyLocal: Marketing Strategy & Script 🚀

This document outlines the core "Need" for the StudyLocal platform and provides high-reach marketing content designed to scale through social media and community word-of-mouth.

---

## 🏗️ The Problem: Why StudyLocal?

### 1. The "Middleman Tax"
Current tutoring agencies take **30% to 50%** of a tutor's first-month salary and often charge parents "registration fees." This inflates the cost for parents and deflates the earnings for educators.
*   **StudyLocal Solution**: ₹0 Commission. We are a search engine, not an agency.

### 2. The "Hyper-Local" Gap
Big-tech apps (Byju's, etc.) push expensive, standardized digital content. But every school has a different curriculum. Parents want someone who knows **their** local school's syllabus.
*   **StudyLocal Solution**: Search by neighbourhood. Find the tutor who lives 2 streets away.

### 3. The Trust Deficit
Online-only tutoring lacks the accountability of a known face.
*   **StudyLocal Solution**: Direct chat and phone calls. Meet the person before you pay. Verified profiles with local bios.

## 🚀 The Hook & The Reach
**The "Reach" Strategy**:
1.  **Community SEO**: We've already implemented `sitemap.ts` and `robots.ts` to capture local searches like "Maths tutor in [Salt Lake/Area]". 
2.  **Hyper-Local Performance**: When a parent sees a "Verified" badge, they don't just see a tutor; they see a local professional.
3.  **The WOW Factor**: Direct Contact. Most platforms hide the phone number behind a paywall. **Giving it away for free is our biggest marketing weapon.**
